## Pronunciation Examples

Here are a few words related to mathematics with their pronunciation:

- **ΜΑΘΗΜΑΤΙΚΗ** (Mathematiki) - *Mathematical*
- **ΛΟΓΙΚΗ** (Logiki) - *Logic*
- **ΓΕΩΜΕΤΡΙΑ** (Geometria) - *Geometry*
- **ΑΡΙΘΜΗΤΙΚΗ** (Arithmitiki) - *Arithmetic*
